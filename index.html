Engineering Intern - Ministry Of Transportation Ontario

Duration: April 2024 - August 2025

At the Ministry of Transportation, I developed Python scripts to extract traffic data from the HERE platform via XML, using Shapely and GeoPandas to clean and process spatial data for accurate route-based analysis.
